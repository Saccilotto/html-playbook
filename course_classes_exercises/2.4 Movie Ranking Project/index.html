My top 3 movies until now

Mushishi

This series/movie is one of a few that keeps you relaxed and entertained at the same time.
It also comes with a great soundtrack and a beautiful art style. It also delivers a sense of mistery
and spirituality that is hard to find in other series.

Wolf Children

This movie is a masterpiece. It's a story about a mother and her children, and how they grow up.
It's a story about love, loss, and how to deal with it. It's a story about life.
The movie can make you truly feel the characters' emotions, and it's a great experience.

Princess Mononoke

A story of an ancient scenario with fantastic creatures and new emerging civilizations.
In this context, we meet a wild girl who grew with the wolves. Her fight for nature and the
spirits of the forest against the corrupting civilization makes for a great plot.
